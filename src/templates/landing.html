Designed for serious investors and traders.

Signal Sentinel doesn't tell you what to buy or sell. It makes sure you never miss the conditions you care about most.

Smart rule engine

Define rules based on price, percentage moves, RSI thresholds, and 52-week levels for individual tickers or your whole portfolio.

Examples: "Alert me if any stock falls 20% below my cost basis" or "Ping me when RSI > 70 on swing positions."

AI-enhanced context

When an alert fires, Signal Sentinel adds a concise AI summary so you see the why: price action, RSI, 52-week context, and sentiment.

No advice-just the data and patterns that matter.

Signal quality tracking

Rate alerts as useful, actionable, or noise. Over time, learn which rules and strategies actually help your decision-making.

Tune your system like a trading algorithm, without writing code.

How Signal Sentinel works

Step 1

Connect or import your portfolio

Import positions from Schwab CSV or connect via Plaid. Your holdings and cost basis stay in your control.

Step 2

Apply strategies or define rules

Use built-in strategy presets like capital preservation, dip hunter, or swing trader-or craft your own custom rules.

Step 3

Let Signal Sentinel watch the markets

The monitor daemon checks your rules on a schedule, sends Telegram alerts, and logs everything in your dashboard.
